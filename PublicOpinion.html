Climate change refers to the long-term change in the average weather patterns that have come to define Earth’s local, regional and global climates according to NASA Before diving into the details, we want to know how the general public feels about climate change so that we could better inform and educate on the topic.

In figure 1, we processed tweets with the keywords “climate change” and their associated sentiment scores to see what people on Twitter think about climate change. Despite the fact that the majority of the population do believe in man-made climate change, 9% of the general public still do not believe in it. In particular, one of the most frequent words in that category is “hoax”.
